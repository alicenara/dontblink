About Stare

Stare is a puzzle horror game set in a mental hospital

You are a security guard trying to escape the hospital. The patients have run amok, being infected by an unknown disease that makes them crazy. When observed by guards or surveillance cameras they are paralyzed

Can you escape the hospital alive?
